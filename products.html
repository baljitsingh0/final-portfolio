Web Design/Development

JHC will design/redesign your website for mobile, tablet and desktop. The website will use responsive design and semantic code. You will be required to answer some questions and give feedback on designs. Additionally, we will be basing the project on the information in the creative brief.

The website design/redesign will include a creative brief, a style guide, target market research analysis, low fidelity, high fidelity prototypes and a final version deployed on your domain. You will also be provided with a final website presentation and analysis.

Logo Design

JHC will design a logo for you based on your specifications. Logo will follow your brand guide/ style guide and you can choose from the different styles of logos such as negative-space logos, text logos, symbol logos and much more. Logo will be in vector format. You will be asked questions to get an idea of what logo would best suit your needs.

Graphic Design

JHC will design pamphlets, banners, assets for your website and vectorize raster images for laser engraving.

Laser Engraving

JHC will laser engrave a vector image on your choice of wood, cork or glass.
